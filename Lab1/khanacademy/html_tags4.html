Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1

Step 1: Mix all ingredients in a bowl.

Step 2: Pour batter onto a hot pan.

Step 3: Cook until golden brown.

Pasta Bolognese

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400 g
Ground beef 300 g
Tomato sauce 400 g
Onion 1
Garlic 2 cloves
Olive oil 2 tbsp

Step 1: Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.

Step 2: Fry onion and garlic in olive oil.

Step 3: Add ground beef and cook until browned.

Step 4: Pour in tomato sauce and simmer for 15–20 minutes.

Step 5: Serve sauce over pasta.

Source: Wikipedia – Bolognese sauce
